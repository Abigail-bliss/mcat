# Phase 1 — Repeating Weekly Template (Mon → Sun)

Use this **every week from ~June 29 until WizePrep ends (~Aug 1)**, once you're caught up.
Working full-time + WizePrep lectures on weekends. The catch-up week (`week-of-2026-06-20.md`) comes
first; this is the steady-state rhythm after it.

**Every-day NON-NEGOTIABLE core (always first):**
➤ AnKing: clear **all due reviews** + take the **50 new** cards. No exceptions, even 1-hour days.

**Weekly targets:** ~100+ UWorld Q · all WizePrep lectures kept current · misses backlog at 0 ·
~4 CARS passages · weakness-first (Bio/Biochem/Orgo get the most time).

---

### MON
- **Core** (AnKing).
- **UWorld:** 20 Q on the current weak topic (tutor/untimed), log every miss.
- If behind on any lecture → catch one up @1.5× + quiz.

### TUE
- **Core.**
- **UWorld:** 20 Q, log misses.
- **CARS:** 2 timed passages.

### WED
- **Core.**
- **UWorld:** 20 Q, log misses.
- **Weak-area mini-review:** 30 min re-reading notes / extra AnKing on whatever questions exposed.

### THU
- **Core.**
- **UWorld:** 20 Q, log misses.
- **CARS:** 2 timed passages.

### FRI
- **Core.**
- **UWorld:** 20 Q, log misses.
- **Error-Log cleanup:** address any "not done" actions from the week (memorize/learn items → confirm
  AnKing covers them; build a card only for a true gap).

### SAT
- **Core.**
- **2 WizePrep lectures + quizzes** (the weekend's assigned chapters), @1.5× with quiz-right-after.
- → **Ping Claude** with the chapters finished → I unsuspend that week's MileDown topics.
- **UWorld:** 15 Q on the new content.

### SUN
- **Core.**
- **2 WizePrep lectures + quizzes.**
- → **Ping Claude** to unsuspend.
- **Weekly review (20 min):** update Error Log, check AnKing stats (due load trending OK?), note what
  slipped, set next week's focus.

---

## Phase 1 milestones
- **~Mid-July (caught up, most content covered):** take **1 Altius full-length** as a checkpoint —
  full review the next day. (Your only FL before PTO; skip it if you're still behind on lectures.)
- **~End of July:** WizePrep first pass DONE on all content. Most B/B + C/P MileDown topics unsuspended.
- **Aug 1 → Phase 2:** PTO begins → switch to the Phase 2 plan (heavy UWorld→AAMC, 2 FLs/week, new-card
  cutoff ~Aug 20). See `study-plan.md` §C.

## If a day goes sideways (ADHD / work blew up)
Do **only the core** (AnKing due + as many of the 50 new as you can) and **5 UWorld questions.**
That keeps both streaks alive. Never let a bad day become a zero day — a 10-minute day still counts.
